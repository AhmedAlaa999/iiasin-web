HydroLoop Basin

Overshot Water Wheel Turbine for Sustainable Energy ⚙️🌊

🌍 About

HydroLoop is a closed-loop water system that demonstrates how flowing water can generate energy. Water is pumped upward and flows down to rotate an overshot turbine, converting motion into usable power.
