Overshot Water Wheel Turbine for Sustainable Energy Generation 🌊⚙️

🌍 About the Project

HydroLoop is a sustainable energy system that uses an overshot water wheel turbine. Water is pumped to a higher level, then flows down to rotate the turbine and generate energy. The system works in a continuous closed loop.
